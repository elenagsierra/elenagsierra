Research

As a researcher, I focus on building AI techniques with beneficial use cases, like improving biodiversity monitoring. I am currently working in the Moi lab at Stanford and Carnegie Science under the supervision of Lauren Gillespie. My main research project looks at new machine learning techniques to improve ground-level plant image classification, specificaly looking at ways to incorporate biology concepts into contrastive learning pair selection. Before my current position, I spent Summer 2021 researching carbon tax policies under David Fedor for the Stanford Precourt Institute for Energy. This research resulted in a literature review and a poster presentation at Stanford ASURPS.
