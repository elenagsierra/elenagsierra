Projects

As an engineering student, I have built many projects. The two projects I had the most creative freedom are Treasure Hunt on a Bare-Metal Raspberry Pi and Arduino Flappy Bird. Alongside these projects, I have made a speaker with an AC to DC converter and a Class D Amplifier, a music player using Digital Systems Design in Veriglog on an FPGA board, and various signal decoding and filtering algorithms.
