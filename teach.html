Teaching

I have taught introductory computer science as a Section Leader at Stanford for almost three years. In this role, I taught weekly 1 hour sections for CS 106A and CS 106B, held office hours, and graded and gave feedback on assignments and exams.

During this time, I have created some general study sheets and lesson plans on concepts like linked lists, recursive backtracking, tree traversals, and more.
