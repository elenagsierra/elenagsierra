Science Communication

At Stanford, I am pursuing a Notation in Science Communication. As a part of this Notation, I build skills to effectively communicate data and scientific findings to a variety of audiences effectively. While I work on a variety of topics, I focus my works on communicating equity gaps in data, algorithms, and opinions in Notation coursework. As a researcher, I focus on building AI techniques with beneficial use cases, like improving biodiversity monitoring. I tend communicate these findings to academic audiences in biology and computer science.
